# 等价无穷小替换公式

$$
\begin{align}
\sin x&\sim x\\
\tan x&\sim x\\
\arcsin x&\sim x\\
\arctan x&\sim x\\
\ln(1+x)&\sim x\\
e^x-1&\sim x\\
a^x-1&\sim x\ln a\\\hline
1-\cos x&\sim\frac12x^2\\\hline
x-\arcsin x&\sim\frac16x^3\\
x-\sin x&\sim\frac16x^3\\
x-\arctan x&\sim\frac13x^3\\
x-\tan x&\sim-\frac13x^3\\
\tan x-\sin x&\sim\frac12x^3
\end{align}
$$
